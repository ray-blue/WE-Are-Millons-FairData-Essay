Why Is Fair Data Important?

Fair Data Principles make data valuable and easier to find through unique identifiers. Hence, making it easier to combine and integrate, thanks to formal shared knowledge representation. Such data can be reused and repurposed and shared. Because machines have the means to understand where the data comes from and what it is about. It also accelerates research, boosts cooperation and facilitates reuse in scientific research. Policy Makers and stakeholders have begun seeing the value and benefits of these principles and are pushing for this new innovation.

What is need for Data Sovereignty?

Firstly let us understand what Data Sovereignty is and how we can change it. Data sovereignty is the concept of information which has been converted and stored in binary, digital form. Which is subject to the laws of the country where you're located. Over the years concerns surrounding data sovereignty relating to privacy and ownership have been on the rise. Governments and organizations have failed to follow regulations and prevent data leaks and breeches. In order to strengthen sovereignty and on setting standards, focusing on these few aspects could be the pieces needed for data sovereignty.

  • Adopting technologies that respect and value personal data. While boosting the growth potential of the digital economy
  • Empowering citizens to be aware of how they act and interact with data.
  • Building a climate-neutral and resource-efficient economy
  • Creating a fair Digital Market where business can compete on equal terms and individuals rights are respected.
  • Improving access to digital goods and services across the world, for consumers, business and individuals.
With enough advocacy, accessibility and user friendly platforms that will allow for seamless migration setting sovereignty standards can definitely become a reality.

Personal Data Freedom.

It's very important for us to distinguish between Privacy and Freedom;

  • Privacy The State of being private; the state of not being seen by others
  • Freedom (uncountable) The state of being free, of not being imprisoned or enslaved (countable) The lack of a specific constraint,or of constraints in general; a state of being free unconstrained.
The age of technology has come to the biggest soft threats. Over the years it has been simple for third-parties to collect data that technology makes transparent from who you called, what you spent and where you drove. Making it very easy to find a pattern in that data that could be false or misleading. Many people have suffered from misinterpreted data that has been collected and verified.
We have to start looking at personal data. As part of our identities, nobody should have access or be allowed to use personal data without an individual's consent.

Fair Data Economy.

For a human driven and fair data economy to work. It should be based on three factors: ethical values, technology, and new business models. With a human -driven approach, individual and social interest should determine the business conditions and data used would be of benefit to individuals and society.
When developing a fair data economy, the aim has been to use existing technologies, operating models and concepts across boundaries between different sectors, However this method may not be sustainable because old infrastructure and policy would still be in use. THe goal should be to enable not only a new data based business but new technologies that are built around FAIR DATA. But also easier digital everyday life. That is based on more efficient and personal management of data.
Moving toward the fair use of data will require sustainable solutions. Organizations that operate responsibly will be able to participate in trust-based data-sharing ecosystems in which the value of data increases. Therefore the use of data is combined with a people oriented approach and model in which different parties have common ground rules for sharing data and using it in services.

Technology usage and maturity.

Web3s focus is to create a fairer, more equitable web experience.Having control over personal data and a decentralized environment. With that being said there are many networks that are developing infrastructure and dApps that are promoting a fairer data economy such as.

  • Fair Data Protocol (FDP) is a data interoperability protocol for dApps that use personal data
  • Fairdrive is decentralised storage on Swarm for dApps offering users privacy, data ownership, and control of their digital identity.
  • Fairdrop is an easy and secure way to send your files. No central server. No tracking. No backdoors.
  • FairOS is poised to be the de-centralised OS that manages the resources of the computers in its network, like storage and compute.
  • Galileo is an open source project that allows you to use, create and modify maps of various participants.
It is still too soon to evaluate whether these networks are mature enough to handle and manage Fair Data.The only way these networks can grow is by usage and promotion.

Fairdrive Protocol Roadmap.

Based on the roadmap, Fairdrive has the potential to promote personal data ownership.In a safe environment whereby users are in control of their personal data . Here is a brief description of the type of environment Fairdrive is working on creating.
  • commonly used data-structures, that give opportunity for a dApp to interpret the produced data of another dApp; and also allows for users to easily transfer their data from one application to another,
  • well defined Personal Data structure, that employs the protocolised data-structures and has a clear mapping to any particular data within the user-namespace,
  • uniform addressing of resources such as users, dApps or content,
  • instantly accessible global decentralized storage that provide meta-information about users, dApps or data-structures,
  • dApp environment and its workflow especially regarding to authentication, authorization and interaction with the personal data of users,
  • web3 backend service architectures that substitute the server side of web2 that aggregate on shared data and serve users in a reliable way.
